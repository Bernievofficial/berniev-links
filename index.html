Artist Bio

↓ ↑

BernieV is a DJ & producer creating electronic, beach-chill and vocal deep house with a warm, emotional feel. Starting on guitar in blues and rock bands, he grew into progressive, deep and tech house, always chasing smooth grooves and soulful vocals.
